About Us

A Message from CEO Mohsin Ali
At Care Plus, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional, affordable healthcare at our state-of-the-art facility in Lahore’s prime location. With advanced diagnostic and therapeutic services across a wide range of specialties, we strive to provide patient-centered care built on the values of integrity, compassion, and ethical excellence. As one of the region’s leading health organizations, we’re committed to promoting wellness and prevention, starting with our very own team. Our mission is to be a positive force for health in the community, empowering our patients and employees alike to live healthier, fulfilling lives.

When you are young and healthy, it never occurs to you that in a single second your whole life could change.
